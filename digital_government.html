Smart Society


Smart Transport


Smart transport, or Intelligent Transport Systems (ITS) use information and communication technologies to digitalise transport infrastructure. An apt example is the use wireless technology to facilitate the exchange of information between vehicles, drivers, passengers and transfer hubs.

Internet For All


'Internet For All' is an initiative to make reliable high speed internet affordable at scale. The core intent is to increase adaptability for the masses which would thereby drive costs south. Multiple Government bodies have set-up initiatives to promote the cause.
